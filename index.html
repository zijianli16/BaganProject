El Capitan, also known as El Cap, is a vertical rock formation in Yosemite National Park, located on the north side of Yosemite Valley.

The granite monolith is about 3,000 feet from base to summit along its tallest face, and is a popular objective for rock climbers.

On June 3, 2017, Alex Honnold made the first free solo ascent of El Capitan, completing the 2,900-foot Freerider route in 3 hours and 56 minutes.
